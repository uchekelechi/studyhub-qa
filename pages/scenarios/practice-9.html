Toast Notifications

Learn: Testing temporary notification messages, auto-dismiss behavior, close buttons.

What to test:

  • Click button to show toast notification
  • Toast appears in top-right corner
  • Toast message displays correct content
  • Toast automatically disappears after 3 seconds
  • Close button (X) dismisses immediately
  • Different types have different colors
